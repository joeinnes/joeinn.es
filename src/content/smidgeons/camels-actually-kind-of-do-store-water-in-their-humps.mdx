summary: Camels actually kind of do store water in their humps
created: 2025-09-02
---
As [Hank Green explains](https://youtube.com/shorts/CEOdmyE-a7M?si=SLWjp1BLja3XfrYN), camels store fat which they can then metabolise with oxygen to create water. And it turns out you get more water that way.
