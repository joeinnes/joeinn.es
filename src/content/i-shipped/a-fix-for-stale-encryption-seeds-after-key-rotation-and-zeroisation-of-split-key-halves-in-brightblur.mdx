After a key rotation in Brightblur, the app was using a cached (stale) encryption seed instead of the new one, requiring a logout to fix. I made the unlock function overwrite the cached seed with the new one immediately after rotation. I also added zeroisation — deliberately overwriting sensitive key material in memory with zeros after it's no longer needed, reducing the window where a key could be extracted.
