Brightblur's dropdown menus were using a global click listener on the entire window to detect outside clicks and close the menu. This listener stayed active even when the menu was closed, creating a leak. I replaced it with a scoped `clickOutside` action that attaches and detaches itself properly, and added Escape key support to dismiss menus too.
