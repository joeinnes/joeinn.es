Brightblur uses on-device face recognition to automatically group photos by person. I found the similarity thresholds used for matching were declared in multiple places and could silently drift apart. I consolidated them into a single source of truth, and fixed several bugs in the recognition pipeline — including one where re-inserting a face into the lookup index could corrupt it.
