Visiting a person's profile page was throwing "Something went wrong" because a cache write was happening inside a Svelte 5 `$derived` reactive expression, which isn't allowed and caused an error. I moved the cache write outside the reactive context. I also fixed the toast notification display, which was rendering too small on some screen sizes.
