I added four accessibility improvements to Brightblur. When navigating between pages, focus now moves to a "skip to main content" target and a screen-reader-friendly route announcement is made. Modal dialogs now trap focus inside them so Tab doesn't escape to the page behind. Unread notification indicators now use shape and label in addition to colour, so they're visible to users who can't distinguish colours.
